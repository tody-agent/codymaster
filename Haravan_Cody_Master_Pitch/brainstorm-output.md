## Phase 1: Discover (Context)
**Target Audience:** Haravan Team (Vietnam-based SaaS company, similar to Shopify).
**Current Pain Points:**
- Traditional Agile (1-3 week sprints) is now too slow for market demands.
- Human testing (QC/QA) is a major bottleneck, too manual and error-prone.
- Code isn't consistently audited or tested thoroughly ("7749 times").
- Too much time required to create "testing concepts" that meet client needs.
- Developers struggle to self-fix bugs efficiently without long feedback loops.

## Phase 2: Define (Qualified Problem)
**For:** The entire Haravan product and engineering organization.
**Who:** Are struggling with slow iteration cycles and manual QA bottlenecks.
**The:** Traditional Agile SDLC is a relic
**That:** Slows down time-to-market and introduces human error.
**Unlike:** Agent-driven development where AI acts as a pair-programmer, automated auditor, and QA.
**Our approach:** Introduce "Cody Master" as a personal, role-based AI companion that integrates directly into their workflows to automate the mundane and elevate the creative.

### Root Cause(s):
1. Over-reliance on human manual QA for deep regression and edge-case testing.
2. Sprint ceremonies and handoffs create unnecessary friction.
3. Lack of automated, pre-commit/pre-merge AI auditing.

## Phase 3: Options Evaluated
1. **Option A: The "Old Friend" Role-Based Playbooks**

2. **Option B: The Traditional Process Manual**
   - A single, massive document outlining the new "Agentic Workflow" to replace Agile. (High friction, low emotional resonance).
